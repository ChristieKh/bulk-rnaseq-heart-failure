# Interpretation notes

## Goal

This file will contain biological interpretation of the main RNA-seq results after differential expression and enrichment analyses are complete.

## Questions to answer later

- Do WT and APP samples separate clearly at the transcriptomic level?
- How many genes are differentially expressed?
- Which genes show the strongest changes?
- Which biological processes are enriched?
- Do the results suggest altered neuronal function, signaling, transport, or stress-related pathways?
- What can be concluded with confidence, and what should remain cautious?

## Interpretation principles

- focus on patterns, not isolated genes only
- connect gene lists to biological functions
- avoid overstating findings
- keep the interpretation proportional to the small sample size
